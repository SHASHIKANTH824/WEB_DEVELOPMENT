Biography of Nikola Tesla


Early life


Nikola Tesla was born on july 10,1856, in smiljan,which was part of the Austrian Empire(modern-day Croatia).
His father was a priest, and his mother was an inventor who encouraged his curiosoty from a young age.





Career


Tesla moved to the united states in 1884 to work for Thomas Edison. He later invented the alternating current(AC) electrical system, which laid the foundation for modern electricity distributions.
He also conducted pioneering experiments in wireless communication and X-rays.





Legacy


Tesla's work has left a lasting impact on modern technology. His inventions have paved the way for many of the electical systems we use today.
He is remembered as a visionary who was ahead of his time.
